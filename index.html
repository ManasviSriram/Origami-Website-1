Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important way of communication among chameleons.
  • Chameleons wrap their tails around trees branches to stick to them.

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are known for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • They have an exellent hearing ability.
